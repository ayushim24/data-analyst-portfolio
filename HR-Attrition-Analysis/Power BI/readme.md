# HR Attrition Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project is an interactive **HR Attrition Analytics Dashboard** built using **Power BI** to analyze employee attrition trends and key HR metrics.

The dashboard provides insights into employee behavior, department-wise attrition, salary distribution, promotions, and work-life balance to support better HR decision-making.

## Objectives
- Analyze employee attrition across different departments and job roles  
- Track important HR KPIs in one place  
- Understand impact of promotions and salary on attrition  
- Compare workforce distribution across departments  
- Enable data-driven HR decisions

## Key KPIs
- **Total Employees:** 50K  
- **Average Income:** 26K  
- **Attrition Count:** 25K  
- **Average Salary Hike:** 24.62  
- **Average Years in Company:** 11

## Dashboard Features

### Slicers (Filters)
- Income Group (High / Medium / Low)  
- Department  
- Job Role  

### Visuals Included

#### Average Hourly Rate by Job Role  
Shows comparison of hourly rate across different job roles.

#### Attrition vs Last Year Promotion  
Analyzes attrition based on years since last promotion.

#### Job Role vs Work-Life Balance  
Displays work-life balance ratings across job roles.

#### Average Working Years by Department  
Shows department-wise employee experience using treemap.

#### Attrition Rate by Department  
Compares attrition count across different departments.

#### Attrition vs Monthly Income  
Shows attrition distribution across income groups.

## Key Insights
- Highest attrition is seen in employees with **1–5 years since last promotion**  
- **Sales department** has slightly higher attrition  
- Attrition is almost equally distributed across income groups  
- Work-life balance is similar across job roles  
- Average experience is consistent across departments
